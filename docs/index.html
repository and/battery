How it works

01

Set your threshold

Choose the battery percentage that should trigger the alarm. Default is 20%.

02

Enable monitoring

Toggle monitoring on. The app listens for battery changes using system-level events.

03

Get alerted

When battery drops below your threshold, a loud alarm sounds with a persistent notification.

04

Plug in to silence

The alarm only stops when you connect your charger. No snooze, no dismiss.
